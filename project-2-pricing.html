Methodology

How It Works

The pipeline ingests lost-deal CRM records and applies spaCy NLP to extract pricing-related sentences from notes. Custom entity recognition identifies competitor names, price figures, and objection language.

Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA) clusters objections into 5 thematic buckets. A logistic regression then models win probability as a function of price tier, controlling for segment, deal size, and rep.

Data Sources
CRM lost-deal records, close reason fields, contact notes, call transcript summaries, discount request logs
NLP Stack
spaCy for entity extraction, scikit-learn LDA for topic modeling, VADER for sentiment on pricing conversations
Output
Pricing kill zones by segment, optimal discount ceiling, and a ranked objection taxonomy the pricing team can act on immediately
