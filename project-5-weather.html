Methodology

How It Works

Three models are stacked: XGBoost predicts close probability for each deal; LSTM models the time-series patterns in pipeline coverage and deal progression; Monte Carlo simulations run 10,000 scenarios by sampling from the joint probability distribution of all open deals.

Storm warnings are generated by an anomaly detection layer (Isolation Forest) that flags deals whose behavioral signals deviate significantly from their recent trajectory — the revenue equivalent of a sudden pressure drop before a storm.

Ensemble Stack
XGBoost (45%) + LSTM (35%) + Linear baseline (20%) — weights determined by out-of-sample RMSE on 6 historical quarters
Uncertainty
Conformal prediction intervals provide statistically valid coverage guarantees — not just "best guess ± something"
Meteorology Analogy
The probability cone is literally borrowed from NOAA hurricane track forecasting. The widening bands communicate growing uncertainty over time — something no CRM dashboard does.
