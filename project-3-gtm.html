Methodology

How It Works

CRM activity data is extracted for all closed-won and closed-lost deals. Each deal's touchpoint history is encoded as an ordered sequence — e.g., ["cold_email", "linkedin", "demo_booked", "exec_call", "proposal", "won"].

Sequential pattern mining (PrefixSpan algorithm) finds the subsequences that appear significantly more often in won deals than lost deals. This is the same algorithm used in clickstream analysis and NLP n-gram extraction.

A transition effectiveness matrix is then built, showing which step-to-step transitions produce the highest win rate lift — helping reps know exactly what to do next after each touchpoint.

Algorithm
PrefixSpan sequential pattern mining — O(n) complexity, scales to millions of activity records from Salesforce or HubSpot
Novel Framing
Treating each deal as a "sentence" of touchpoints — with order and context mattering — rather than counting channel touches independently
Business Output
A ranked "play book" of winning sequences, a "next best action" transition matrix, and per-segment sequence effectiveness scores
